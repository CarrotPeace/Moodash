Moodash: Amazing Mood Tracker App

1. An Overview of the proposed Android app

The importance of emotional health is noticed by more and more people, and they are adopting the practice of tracking their moods on a daily basis to raise the awareness of their emotional state and enable effective management of their mental health. Therefore, our group is intended to explore the technology and feasible approaches in Android applications that are able to make a contribution in the field of mental health and emotional regulation. To this end, we plan to develop an application that allows users to record their feelings on a daily basis that enables users to consciously manage their emotions. Ultimately, our goal is to help the public to understand, perceive, and manage their emotions more effectively, leading to a more promising and vigorous life.

2. User group

Our software is designed for everyone, and we hope to encourage more people to actively manage their emotional health by simply recording their feelings. For those who are aware of their emotional issues, our software could be regarded as a tracking tool that provides meaningful aphorism to help users to monitor their emotional changes. For the people who are interested in mental health and emotional regulation, our software serves as a concrete tool for mental health education, helping users to develop emotional awareness and mental health regulation skills through simple functions. Additionally, our software can be used as a record book for anyone who wants to mark and remember the events that trigger their deep feeling, preserving their valuable memories for years to come.

3. Key functionalities

Mood Recording and Quotes Pushing

The user will select their current emotion from a list of pre-set emotions in the app, then the app will use an API to access a database of quotes and select the appropriate quote based on the user's emotion. Also, the app will access the user's location for functionality.

Quotes Collection

The user will have the option to "like" the quote they receive in functionality 1, and the app will save it to their quotes collection. The user can view their saved quotes in a separate section.

Personal Data Analysis and Google Calendar

This functionality allows users to choose a certain date period and view an analysis of their recorded emotions.

Map Showing Moods by Region

This functionality allows users to view a map showing the recorded emotions of app users in different regions. The app will access the recorded emotions of app users in functionality 1 and display the number of each emotion in a certain area using different icons.
